Gram's Milk Tea Bar

The Gram's Milk Tea Bar was founded in Norzagaray, Bulacan by Gillianne Santiago, Ruby Ann Agustin, Angelica Yambot and Mariz Respicio.

In addition to our milk tea menu, we serve good side dishes.

"Use products from nature for what it's worth - but never too early, nor too late." Fresh is the new sweet.

Chef, Milk Tea Lover and Co-Owner: Ruby Ann Agustin

Opening hours: Everyday from 10:00 am to 10:00 pm.

Address: Norzagaray, Bulacan
